7 act structure with sub acts
format script this way

Act 1 (5 minutes)
Sub-act 1: Introduce Protagonist (1 minute)

Matt, a struggling writer, sits at his desk.
Sub-act 2: Establish Stakes (2 minutes)

Matt's deadline is looming, and he's struggling to write.
Sub-act 3: Inciting Incident (2 minutes)

Matt meets with his friend, Sarah, who suggests a new idea.
Act 2 (8 minutes)
Sub-act 1: Explore New Idea (1.5 minutes)

Matt and Sarah brainstorm.
Sub-act 2: Obstacles Arise (1.5 minutes)

Matt faces writer's block.
Sub-act 3: Plot Twist (1 minute)

Matt discovers a hidden talent.
Sub-act 4: Raise Stakes (1 minute)

Matt's publisher wants a new manuscript.
Sub-act 5: Character Development (1.5 minutes)

Matt learns to trust himself.
Sub-act 6: Midpoint Revelation (1 minute)

Matt realizes his true passion.
Act 3 (8 minutes)
Sub-act 1: Newfound Inspiration (1.5 minutes)

Matt writes with renewed purpose.
Sub-act 2: Challenges Mount (1.5 minutes)

Matt faces criticism.
Sub-act 3: Character Growth (1 minute)

Matt develops confidence.
Sub-act 4: Plot Thickens (1 minute)

Matt's story takes a surprising turn.
Sub-act 5: Relationships Deepen (1.5 minutes)

Matt and Sarah's friendship grows.
Sub-act 6: Complications Arise (1 minute)

Matt's publisher has doubts.
Act 4 (8 minutes)
Sub-act 1: Turning Point (1.5 minutes)

Matt's manuscript is rejected.
Sub-act 2: Emotional Shift (1.5 minutes)

Matt feels defeated.
Sub-act 3: New Perspective (1 minute)

Matt re-evaluates his work.
Sub-act 4: Resolve Strengthened (1 minute)

Matt decides to self-publish.
Sub-act 5: Action Plan (1.5 minutes)

Matt outlines a new strategy.
Sub-act 6: Momentum Builds (1 minute)

Matt gains support.
Act 5 (8 minutes)
Sub-act 1: Self-Doubt (1.5 minutes)

Matt questions his decision.
Sub-act 2: External Pressures (1.5 minutes)

Financial struggles.
Sub-act 3: Creative Block (1 minute)

Matt struggles to write.
Sub-act 4: Support System (1 minute)

Sarah encourages Matt.
Sub-act 5: Breakthrough (1.5 minutes)

Matt finds inspiration.
Sub-act 6: Resolve Strengthened (1 minute)

Matt perseveres.
Act 6 (8 minutes)
Sub-act 1: Final Push (1.5 minutes)

Matt completes his manuscript.
Sub-act 2: Confronting Fears (1.5 minutes)

Matt faces his critics.
Sub-act 3: Triumph (1 minute)

Matt's work is recognized.
Sub-act 4: Emotional Release (1 minute)

Matt feels proud.
Sub-act 5: New Beginnings (1.5 minutes)

Matt's career takes off.
Sub-act 6: Reflection (1 minute)

Matt reflects on his journey.
Act 7 (8 minutes)
Sub-act 1: New Chapter (2 minutes)

Matt starts a new project.
Sub-act 2: Closure (2 minutes)

Matt says goodbye to his old life.
Sub-act 3: Future Prospects (4 minutes)

Matt looks forward to his future.
