# Commercial Deck

## Purpose
This deck is designed to manage and schedule commercial breaks, advertisements, and promotional content for a broadcast. It ensures timely and accurate playback of commercial spots.

## Key Features
*   **Commercial Scheduling:** Plan and schedule commercial breaks with specific ad spots.
*   **Ad Rotation:** Automate the rotation of different advertisements to ensure fair play and maximize exposure.
*   **Spot Management:** Organize and categorize commercial spots by length, product, or campaign.
*   **Real-time Ad Insertion:** Dynamically insert ads based on live audience data or program content.
*   **Reporting and Analytics:** Track ad performance, play counts, and revenue generation.
*   **Compliance Tools:** Ensure adherence to broadcasting regulations and advertising standards.
