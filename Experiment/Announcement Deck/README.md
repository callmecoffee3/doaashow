# Announcement Deck

## Purpose
This deck is designed to manage and broadcast important announcements and messages. It provides a structured way to deliver information to the audience or team members.

## Key Features
*   **Announcement Scheduling:** Plan and schedule when announcements should be played.
*   **Message Templates:** Create and store pre-defined announcement templates for common messages.
*   **Urgent Announcement Override:** Instantly broadcast urgent or emergency announcements.
*   **Multi-channel Delivery:** Send announcements to different audio channels or displays.
*   **Announcement History:** Maintain a log of all played announcements.
*   **Manager Notification:** Automatically notify managers or supervisors when specific announcements are made.
