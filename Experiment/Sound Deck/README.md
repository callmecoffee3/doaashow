# Sound Deck

## Purpose
This deck is dedicated to managing and playing various sound effects, jingles, and audio cues during a broadcast or live event. It provides quick access to a library of sounds to enhance the production.

## Key Features
*   **Sound Library:** Organize and categorize a vast collection of sound effects, jingles, and musical stings.
*   **Instant Playback:** Trigger sounds instantly with dedicated buttons or hotkeys.
*   **Looping and One-Shot Modes:** Play sounds once or loop them continuously.
*   **Volume and Pan Control:** Adjust the volume and stereo positioning of individual sounds.
*   **Sound Layers:** Combine multiple sounds to create complex audio landscapes.
*   **Search and Filter:** Quickly find specific sounds within the library.
*   **Custom Sound Upload:** Allow users to upload and integrate their own sound files.
