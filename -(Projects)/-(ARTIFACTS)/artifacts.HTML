HBO

“It’s Not TV. It’s HBO.”

Overview

Type: Entertainment Network
Parent Company: Warner Bros. Discovery
Founded: Nov 8, 1972
Founder: Charles Dolan
Origin: Wilkes-Barre, PA

Business Model

Revenue: Subscription fees via cable tiers and Max streaming tiers.

Strategy: High-budget, creator-driven premium programming emphasizing quality over quantity.

Signature Programming

Drama

  • The Sopranos
  • The Wire
  • Succession
  • Game of Thrones
  • The Last of Us

Comedy

  • Sex and the City
  • Curb Your Enthusiasm
  • Veep
  • Silicon Valley
  • Barry

Limited Series

  • Band of Brothers
  • Chernobyl
  • The White Lotus
  • Watchmen

Historical Milestones

1972: Launched as the first premium cable television network.
1975: First network to transmit via satellite (Thrilla in Manila boxing).
1999: Debuted 'The Sopranos', launching the Golden Age of TV.
2015: Launched HBO Now, bypassing traditional cable requirements.
2023: Rebranded the main streaming ecosystem to 'Max'.
